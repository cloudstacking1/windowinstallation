      Types of Windows We Install

      At Deluxe Replacement Windows, we offer a wide selection of house windows, home windows, and office windows to suit every style and need:

      Double Hung Windows

      Double hung windows are among the most popular choices for residential window installation. They offer timeless appeal, excellent ventilation, and easy cleaning.

      Bow Windows

      If you’re looking to add elegance and extra space, bow windows are a perfect solution. They project outward from your home, creating a beautiful curve that brings in natural light.

      Bay Windows

      Similar to bow windows but with sharper angles, bay windows create cozy interior spaces like reading nooks while enhancing your home’s curb appeal.

      Vinyl Windows

      Vinyl windows are a popular choice for homeowners looking for energy efficiency, durability, and low maintenance. Our vinyl options come in various colors and styles to match any design.

      Aluminum Windows

      For those who prefer a modern, industrial aesthetic, aluminum windows offer strength, slim profiles, and sleek appearances, perfect for commercial and residential properties alike.

      Casement Windows

      Casement windows open outward with a crank, offering superior airflow and unobstructed views — a great choice for hard-to-reach areas like over kitchen sinks.

      Sliding Windows

      Sliding windows glide open horizontally, making them ideal for wide window openings where vertical space is limited.
